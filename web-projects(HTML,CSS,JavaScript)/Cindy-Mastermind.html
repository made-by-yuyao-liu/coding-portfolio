Play Mastermind Online!
Object of the Game

To outsmart the computer with great guesswork.

Your Goal

As the Decoder: you must break the secret code in the fewest number of guesses.

Game Play
  1. 1. Click on   Start New Game  .
  2. 2. Begin guessing, try to duplicate the exact colors and positions of the hidden Code pegs.
    Note: there are no duplicate colors.
  3. 3. Each guess is made by clicking a peg in the selection area, then clicking an empty spot in the guess row.
  4. 4. After every guess, the computer will inform you of your progress through the little dots on the right.
Red Dots

The red dots shows the number of pegs that are the correct colors in the correct positions.

White Dots

White dots show the number of pegs that are the correct colors but in incorrect positions.

Example

In this case the player has 1 peg that is the correct colors in the correct position and 2 pegs that are the same color but in incorrect positions. Therefore there are 1 red dots and 2 white dots.
